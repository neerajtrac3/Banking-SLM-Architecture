# Banking SLM Use Cases  
### High‑Precision, Low‑Latency, Regulated‑Industry Applications  
### Author: Neeraj Aggarwal

Small Language Models (SLMs) unlock a new class of AI capabilities for banking by combining domain‑specific training, deterministic behavior, ultra‑low latency, and strict regulatory alignment. This document outlines the most critical Banking SLM use cases across payments, custody, lending, liquidity, compliance, and modernization.

These use cases are powered by ERMI (risk scoring), UICS (context stitching), and UAMMF (model governance), ensuring safe deployment across Tier‑0/Tier‑1 systems.

---

## 1. Payments Intelligence & ISO 20022 Reasoning

### **Use Case**
- Interpret, validate, and transform ISO 20022 payment messages  
- Detect anomalies in payment routing  
- Provide deterministic reasoning for high‑volume payment flows  

### **SLM Advantage**
- Domain‑specific vocabulary  
- Zero hallucinations in Tier‑0  
- <50ms inference for real‑time payments  

### **Example Tasks**
- Validate PACS.008, PACS.009, CAMT messages  
- Identify missing mandatory fields  
- Suggest corrections aligned with SWIFT/ISO rules
